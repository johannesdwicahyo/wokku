# Dyno Tiers

Choose the right resources for your app. Available on [wokku.dev](https://wokku.dev) managed cloud.

## Plans

| Tier | RAM | vCPU | Storage | Price |
|------|-----|------|---------|-------|
| **Free** | 256 MB | 0.15 | — | Free |
| **Basic** | 512 MB | 0.3 | 512 MB | $1.50/mo |
| **Standard** | 1 GB | 0.5 | 1 GB | $4/mo |
| **Performance** | 2 GB | 1.0 | 2 GB | $8/mo |
| **Performance 2x** | 4 GB | 2.0 | 5 GB | $15/mo |

## Free Tier

- Sleeps after 30 minutes of inactivity (Eco dynos)
- Wakes on next request (cold start ~5 seconds)
- Great for development and side projects

## Changing Tiers

Change your dyno tier from the Web UI: go to your app → **Scaling → Change Tier**.

## Self-Hosted

If you self-host Wokku (CE), there are no tiers or billing — you manage resources directly on your servers.
