# Plans & Pricing

## Managed Cloud (wokku.dev)

| Tier | RAM | vCPU | Storage | Price |
|------|-----|------|---------|-------|
| **Free** | 256 MB | 0.15 | — | Free |
| **Basic** | 512 MB | 0.3 | 512 MB | $1.50/mo |
| **Standard** | 1 GB | 0.5 | 1 GB | $4/mo |
| **Performance** | 2 GB | 1.0 | 2 GB | $8/mo |
| **Performance 2x** | 4 GB | 2.0 | 5 GB | $15/mo |

Databases are included at no extra cost. You only pay for app dynos.

## Free Tier

- 256 MB RAM, 0.15 vCPU
- Eco dynos (sleep after 30 min idle)
- Unlimited apps (within resource limits)
- 1 server connection
- Perfect for side projects and development

## Self-Hosted (Free)

Self-host Wokku on your own servers for free. The Community Edition is open source under AGPL-3.0.

```bash
git clone https://github.com/johannesdwicahyo/wokku.git
cd wokku && docker compose up -d
```

No limits, no billing, full control. You manage your own infrastructure.
