# Usage

Track your resource usage and manage billing.

## View Usage

Go to **Settings → Billing** in the dashboard to see:

- Current plan per app
- Monthly cost breakdown
- Resource usage (RAM, CPU)
- Billing cycle dates

## How Billing Works

- Billed hourly, charged monthly
- Prorated when you upgrade or downgrade mid-cycle
- Free tier is always free — no credit card required

## Upgrade / Downgrade

Change your dyno tier anytime from the app's **Scaling** page. Changes take effect immediately.

## Payment Methods

- Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, AMEX)
- Payment processed via iPaymu
