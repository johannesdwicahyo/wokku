## TL;DR Strategy

### Who you are
15-year senior developer who mostly freelanced, had no online presence, and just started building products solo with AI agents.

### What you post
1. **Agentic engineering techniques** (40%) — how you actually use Claude Code
2. **Veteran perspective** (25%) — 15 years of context applied to modern dev
3. **Building in public** (25%) — Wokku progress, real numbers, real failures
4. **Tool reviews** (10%) — honest comparisons, high SEO value

### Where you post
- **TikTok + Instagram Reels + YouTube Shorts** — primary, 3x/week (cross-posted)
- **Twitter/X** — secondary, 3-5 tweets/week + 1 thread
- **LinkedIn** — occasional, 1 post/week
- **YouTube long-form** — Month 2+, 1-2 videos/month

### Time commitment
~12 hours/week total:
- 4-6 hours content creation
- 7 hours engagement/replies
- Sustainable alongside Wokku development

## Week 1 Launch Sequence

### Day 1: Setup (2 hours)
- [ ] Order Boya BY-M1 lavalier mic (Rp 100-150k)
- [ ] Download CapCut on phone
- [ ] Create/update accounts: TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Twitter, LinkedIn
- [ ] Update bios on all 5 platforms (templates in doc 01)
- [ ] Delete old content on Instagram/TikTok (or archive it)

### Day 2-3: Learn recording (2-3 hours)
- [ ] Mic arrives, test audio
- [ ] Record practice takes of Script #1 (don't post)
- [ ] Review playback, adjust mic position
- [ ] Edit a practice video in CapCut

### Day 4: Record the first video (1-2 hours)
- [ ] Read the "first video script" (doc 02) aloud 3 times
- [ ] Record it 3-5 times
- [ ] Pick the best take
- [ ] Edit in CapCut (add captions, music, transitions)
- [ ] Review once, sleep on it

### Day 5: Post it (30 min)
- [ ] Watch your video fresh in the morning
- [ ] If it's okay (not perfect — okay), post to TikTok at 20:00 WIB
- [ ] Post first tweet introducing yourself
- [ ] Don't check metrics for 24 hours

### Day 6-7: Record batch (2-3 hours)
- [ ] Record Scripts #1, #13, #7 in one session
- [ ] These are your videos for next week
- [ ] Edit them over the weekend
- [ ] Schedule in Buffer or manually post Monday/Wednesday/Friday

## The Core Commitment

**90 days. 3 videos per week. No breaks.**

That's the minimum viable content strategy. Anything less won't produce results. Anything more is burnout risk.

### What you'll have after 90 days
- 30+ videos published
- 500-2,000 followers across platforms
- First case studies of Wokku users
- Skill in creating content
- Habit of shipping consistently

### What you'll miss if you stop early
- Algorithm doesn't trust you yet
- No compounding from repeated exposure
- Audience never grows large enough to matter
- All content work wasted

**Consistency compounds. Don't break the chain.**

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Over-producing first videos** — perfect quality, zero consistency. Post the mediocre version, improve over time.

2. **Waiting for the "right" topic** — the best topic is the one you'll actually record today.

3. **Comparing to big creators** — they started where you are. Compare to yourself 30 days ago.

4. **Apologizing for English** — your audience finds authenticity, not perfection.

5. **Checking metrics hourly** — checks every 24 hours at most. Hourly checking kills motivation.

6. **Deleting "bad" videos** — low-performing videos still exist, still get indexed, still compound. Leave them.

7. **Switching strategies weekly** — give each format 10-15 videos before deciding it doesn't work.

8. **Neglecting Wokku for content** — content is support function for the product. Don't swap.

9. **Not engaging with comments** — responding builds community. Not responding kills it.

10. **Trying every platform at once** — master TikTok first, then add others.

## Red Flags to Watch For

### Day 30 checkpoint
If you see:
- ❌ Under 100 followers across all platforms → content isn't finding audience, change format
- ❌ No Wokku signups from content → CTAs aren't working, rework
- ❌ Zero engagement → algorithm isn't promoting, post at different times

### Day 60 checkpoint
- ❌ Still under 300 followers → maybe personal brand isn't your channel, pivot to paid ads
- ❌ No repeat viewers → content isn't sticky, find what resonates
- ❌ No comments/questions → too broad or too technical, find middle ground

### Day 90 checkpoint
If you're significantly below targets:
- Reassess honestly: is content your channel?
- Alternative: focus Wokku time on product + run paid ads
- Personal brand can be Year 2, not Year 1

If you're significantly above:
- Go harder, more content
- Maybe quit consulting to go full-time
- Start thinking about second product
