# Wokku — Month 1 Content Scripts (30 videos)

> All scripts in Bahasa Indonesia, optimized for TikTok/Instagram Reels (15-60 seconds).
> Format: screen recording + voiceover or text overlay.

---

## Content Strategy

**Core format:** "X di Wokku dalam Y detik"
- Show the thing working, not the thing being sold
- Real screen recording, real deploy, real result
- Casual tone, zero marketing-speak
- Focus on proof, not promises

**Hook rules (first 3 seconds):**
- Start with action or surprising claim
- No intro, no logo reveal
- Bahasa Indonesia santai (not formal)

## Production Notes

### Recording setup (minimum viable)
- **Phone** with tripod
- **Lavalier mic** (Rp 100-200k) — critical for audio quality
- **Good lighting** (face by a window works)
- **Screen recording:** QuickTime (Mac) or OBS (Windows)
- **Editing:** CapCut (free, Indonesian creators' favorite)

### Batch recording strategy
Don't record 1 per day. Batch:
- **Monday**: Record 5 scripts in 2 hours
- **Tuesday**: Edit 5 scripts in 1 hour
- **Wednesday**: Schedule all 5 in Buffer, ready to post Thu-Mon

### Format variations to test
1. **Voiceover + screen record** (easiest, for technical demos)
2. **Face on camera + cutaway to screen** (best for founder stories)
3. **Text overlay + screen record** (no voice, for silent scrollers)

Test all 3 in week 1. Whichever gets highest retention, double down on.
